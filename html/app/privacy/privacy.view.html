How does Classic Collage secure your information?

Your account is protected by your App ID. This app id is yours and after you sign up for the SDK you will receive an email with an app ID. You will have to enter this to get started.

Along with the APP ID a,Client Key is generated for you. With this key you can start using the SDK. Both of these are your personal gateways. The client key is sent to the email you signed up with.

You may request to opt-out of marketing related emails but you may not opt out of receiving technical emails around SDK improvements.
